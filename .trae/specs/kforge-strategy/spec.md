# KForge Product Strategy and Market Positioning Spec

## Why
KForge currently has a solid technical foundation (React frontend, Markdown knowledge base, Docker simulations, and automated data integration), but lacks a formalized business and product strategy document. The user has provided a comprehensive market analysis, unique value propositions (UVP), competitive landscape analysis, and a 12-month roadmap. Formalizing this into the project's documentation will guide future development, marketing, and monetization efforts, ensuring the project aligns with the needs of its target audience (25-35-year-old cybersecurity professionals).

## What Changes
- Create a new strategic planning document `docs/STRATEGY.md` containing the market analysis, UVPs, competitive analysis, and 12-month roadmap provided by the user.
- Add a reference to this new strategy document in the main `README.md`.

## Impact
- Affected specs: None (this is a new strategic document).
- Affected code: `README.md` will be updated to link to the new documentation.

## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Product Strategy Documentation
The repository SHALL contain a formalized strategy document outlining the product's market positioning, unique value propositions, competitive analysis, and future roadmap.

#### Scenario: Success case
- **WHEN** a contributor or investor reviews the repository
- **THEN** they can find `docs/STRATEGY.md` and clearly understand the project's target audience, differentiation strategy, and 12-month growth plan.
